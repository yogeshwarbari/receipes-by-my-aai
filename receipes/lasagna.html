Ingredients

  • Tomatoes - 2
  • Potatoes - 5
  • Onions - 1
  • chili powder - 50 gms/ 2 teaspoon

Steps

  1. Put a frying pan on medium flame gas stove.
  2. Put 2 teaspoon of oil in the pan & wait for 20 seconds.
  3. Fine chop all tomatoes, potatoes and onions then put that in the pan now.
  4. Put a lid on the pan and allow to cook for 20 minutes.
  5. Now remove lid and mix chili powder & garam masala which we didn't tell you in Ingredients.
  6. Serve hot in a bowl with hot chapati or bread.
